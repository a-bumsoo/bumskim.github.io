Super Smash Bros. 4 characters BST

I made a little app in C++ that puts Super Smash Bros. 4 data into a Binary Search Tree, and then lets you, the user, search a character rank and the universe they’re from. You can also find a character by searching their rank.

Before doing any of that, I made a small Python script to scrape the Super Smash Bros. 4 data and put it all in a csv.
